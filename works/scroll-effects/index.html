Section 1

Sections reveal themselves as you scroll down the page. This effect is made possible by the ScrollReveal JavaScript library.

The ScrollReveal library is simple to use, and offers some customization options to control things like durations and delays of reveals, and from where and how they animate in.

The scroll bar at the top of the page, as well as the document background color change depending on your scroll position (using some custom JavaScript written from scratch without a library.
